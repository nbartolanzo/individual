New York Mets

The New York Mets is one of the Major League Baseball teams that is located in New York City. They are a team with a lot of potential to win and a good pitching staff with potential. The team has a lot of heart and the fanbase is very supportive, even in times of losing.

This website is a page for Mets fans by mets fans. Mets fans of all ages can come here to find information about the current team, such as stats and team schedule. For entertainment, fans can watch some videos on the highlights portion of the webpage. These hihglights can consist of some at-bats by some players and some highlights of recent games. Finally, Mets fans can read up on the history of the team and learn about how they came about.

Although they are in a tough stretch of games, the Mets always find a way to get back into the playoffs. That is why they earned the nickname, the Amazin's.
